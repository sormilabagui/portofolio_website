About Me
Designing and Exploring is My Passion

I’m Sormila Bagui, currently pursuing my MCA and I’ve completed my BCA as well. I come from a strong academic background with a CGPA of 8.59 in BCA and 8.80 in my current MCA semester. I enjoy working on creative and logical challenges — my interest in tech blends both. Recently, I created a Sweet Shop Management System using Python with TDD, and a To-Do List App in React using hooks and components. Outside of academics, I love digital art and storytelling. It helps me explore UI/UX design and understand how people interact with technology. I also read psychology and fiction books that shape how I think and communicate. I’m constantly learning, and what excites me most about software development is building real things from ideas — and improving with every attempt. I’m looking forward to opportunities where I can grow while contributing meaningfully.”
